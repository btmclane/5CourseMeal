Easter Brunch at Braden's House

Start with a cottontail cocktail that consists of:

    Marshmallow fluff
    Sweetened shredded coconut
    Tequila, rum, triple sec
    Pineapple and Lime Juice

Start off with some calamari which is squid from the most exotic oceans. Comes with peppers, lemon, and cocktail sauce.

The main course will be some grill cooked filet mignon. This is a leaner meat of steak and has prized texture.

The side dish for this meal is a baked potato topped with:

    Butter, Sour Cream
    Cheese, Bacon bits, and Green onion

The dessert will consist of molten chocolate lava cake.
The ingredients are:

    Eggs, Vanilla, and Egg yolks
    Chocolate, butter, and Cocoa Powder
